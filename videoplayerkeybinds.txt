========================================================================
                    VIDEO PLAYER KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
========================================================================

This document lists all keyboard shortcuts available in the video players.
These keybinds are not customizable through this file - it is for reference only.

========================================================================
                    STANDARD VIDEO PLAYER KEYBINDS
========================================================================

PLAYBACK CONTROLS:
------------------
• Space                  - Play/Pause video
• Escape                 - Exit fullscreen (if in fullscreen) or close player

SEEKING:
--------
• Left Arrow             - Seek backward 10 seconds
• Right Arrow            - Seek forward 10 seconds
• Ctrl + Left Arrow      - Jump to beginning of video
• Ctrl + Right Arrow     - Jump to end of video

VOLUME:
-------
• Up Arrow               - Increase volume by 5%
• Down Arrow             - Decrease volume by 5%
• Mouse Wheel Up         - Increase volume
• Mouse Wheel Down       - Decrease volume

PLAYBACK SPEED:
---------------
• Ctrl + Up Arrow        - Increase playback speed (next preset)
• Ctrl + Down Arrow      - Decrease playback speed (previous preset)

DISPLAY:
--------
• F11                    - Toggle fullscreen mode
• Escape                 - Exit fullscreen mode


========================================================================
                      VR VIDEO PLAYER KEYBINDS
========================================================================

PLAYBACK CONTROLS:
------------------
• Shift + Space          - Play/Pause video
• Ctrl + Space           - Play/Pause video
• Space (Hold)           - Continuously recenter VR view (when in VR mode)
• Escape                 - Close VR player

SEEKING:
--------
• A or Left Arrow        - Seek backward 10 seconds
• D or Right Arrow       - Seek forward 10 seconds
• Shift + A              - Seek backward 60 seconds
• Shift + D              - Seek forward 60 seconds
• Ctrl/Shift + Left      - Seek backward 60 seconds
• Ctrl/Shift + Right     - Seek forward 60 seconds

ZOOM CONTROLS:
--------------
• W or Up Arrow          - Zoom in (increase video scale)
• S or Down Arrow        - Zoom out (decrease video scale)

PLAYBACK SPEED:
---------------
• Shift + W              - Increase playback speed
• Shift + S              - Decrease playback speed
• Ctrl/Shift + Up        - Increase playback speed
• Ctrl/Shift + Down      - Decrease playback speed
• Tab                    - Reset playback speed to 1x
• End                    - Reset playback speed to 1x

WINDOWS SYSTEM VOLUME:
----------------------
• Q or Page Down         - Decrease Windows system volume
• E or Page Up           - Increase Windows system volume


========================================================================
                     VR CONTROLLER BINDINGS
========================================================================

BASIC CONTROLS:
----------------
• Menu/Application Button     - Play/Pause video
• Trigger (Hold)              - Continuously recenter VR view
• Grip Button                 - Modifier key (hold for advanced functions)

TRACKPAD/JOYSTICK CONTROLS:
----------------------------
For Vive/Windows MR (Trackpad - requires CLICK):
• Trackpad Click + Left/Right    - Seek backward/forward 10 seconds
• Trackpad Click + Up/Down       - Zoom in/out (increase/decrease scale)

For Oculus/Index (Joystick - no click required):
• Joystick Left/Right            - Seek backward/forward 10 seconds
• Joystick Up/Down               - Zoom in/out (increase/decrease scale)

GRIP MODIFIER COMBINATIONS:
----------------------------
Hold Grip + other buttons for advanced functions:
• Grip + Trigger                 - Decrease playback speed
• Grip + Menu/Application        - Increase playback speed
• Grip + Trackpad/Joystick Left  - Seek backward 60 seconds
• Grip + Trackpad/Joystick Right - Seek forward 60 seconds
• Grip + Trackpad/Joystick Up    - Increase Windows system volume
• Grip + Trackpad/Joystick Down  - Decrease Windows system volume

CONTROLLER NOTES:
-----------------
• Both left and right controllers work identically
• Trackpad controls require clicking (not just touching) on Vive/WMR
• Joystick controls activate with movement alone on Oculus/Index
• Dead zones prevent accidental input from small movements
• Seek/zoom actions trigger on threshold crossing, not continuous
• Grip must be held before pressing other buttons for combinations


========================================================================
                           ADDITIONAL NOTES
========================================================================

1. The Standard Video Player is used for regular video playback
2. The VR Video Player is specifically for VR/360/180-degree videos
3. In fullscreen mode, moving the mouse will show/hide controls
4. Controls auto-hide after 3 seconds of inactivity in fullscreen
5. Volume can be adjusted up to 200% in the Standard Video Player
6. VR mode requires SteamVR to be installed and running
